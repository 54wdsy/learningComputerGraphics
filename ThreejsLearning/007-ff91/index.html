Dimensions

FF 91's 126-inch long wheelbase and extended platform is a new benchmark in comfort and refinement. The welcome absence of a gas powertrain allowed us to create a unique interior architecture that maximizes space and luxury.

Battery

Our 130 kWh lithium-ion battery pack achieves the world’s highest energy density of any automotive battery currently in production. This enables a tremendous increase in range, well in excess of 300 miles on a single charge and 200 miles on a 30-minute fast charge.

4-Wheel Steering

This feature allows all four wheels to act in unison to give FF 91 incredibly responsive and nimble handling, comparable with the turning radius of a much smaller vehicle.

Aerodynamics

Most luxury vehicles of this size and shape have a drag coefficient in excess of 0.3. Our target is to get it below 0.26. At highway speeds, this 13% reduction in aerodynamic drag gives you a 5% improvement in range.
